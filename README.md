# Hailuo 3.0: The Next Generation Video Foundation Model

**Hailuo 3.0** is the latest, high-fidelity AI video generation model from MiniMax, succeeding the industry-leading **Hailuo 2.3**. 

Designed for theatrical-grade smoothness and full cinematic production, Hailuo 3.0 introduces native 4K resolution, integrated spatial audio, and advanced character persistence.

---

## Evolution: From Hailuo 2.x to Hailuo 3.0

### Previous Model: Hailuo 2.3 (Late 2025)
Hailuo 2.3 was renowned for its superior physics and realistic human motion:
- **Micro-expressions:** Refined facial emotions and subtle muscular movements.
- **Stylization Stability:** Support for diverse artistic styles like Anime, Ink Wash, and Game CG.
- **1080p Standard:** High-definition output at 24-30 fps.
- **Fast Mode:** High-throughput generation for rapid prototyping.

### Next Generation: Hailuo 3.0 (Early 2026)
Hailuo 3.0 bridges the gap between silent AI clips and true cinematic storytelling:

#### 1. Native 4K at 60FPS
A massive jump in quality, offering ultra-high definition and buttery smooth motion, suitable for large-screen cinematic experiences.

#### 2. Synchronized Native Audio & Dialogue
Hailuo 3.0 generates perfectly timed sound effects, ambient environmental audio, and character dialogue that is natively lip-synced to the generated video.

#### 3. Extended Narratives (Up to 30 Seconds)
While previous versions were limited to 6-10 seconds, Hailuo 3.0 supports continuous clips of up to **30 seconds**, allowing for more complex multi-step logical transitions.

#### 4. Advanced Motion Brush & Trajectory
New "Director Mode" tools allow creators to "paint" specific movement onto objects and define precise camera trajectories (push, pull, tilt, orbit) with granular control.

#### 5. Enhanced Spatial Awareness & Physics
A significant upgrade to the underlying physics engine, offering better handling of complex multi-object interactions and consistent lighting/shadows in 3D space.
